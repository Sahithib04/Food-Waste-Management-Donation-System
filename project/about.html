About FoodShare

Learn about our mission to reduce food waste and fight hunger.

Our Mission

At FoodShare, we're committed to creating a sustainable solution to two critical problems: food waste and food insecurity. Our platform connects restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals with excess food to local NGOs and people in need.

Every year, billions of pounds of food go to waste while millions of people go hungry. We believe technology can bridge this gap by making it easier to donate surplus food before it's thrown away.

Through our platform, we aim to:

  • Reduce food waste by at least 30% in communities we serve
  • Provide nutritious meals to those experiencing food insecurity
  • Build stronger community connections between businesses and local organizations
  • Promote environmental sustainability by reducing landfill waste
