Donation Guidelines

Acceptable Foods

  • Unopened packaged foods within expiry date
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Bakery items (less than 1 day old)
  • Cooked food (prepared within 24 hours)
  • Frozen foods (maintained at proper temperature)

Unacceptable Foods

  • Expired food items
  • Food with signs of spoilage
  • Partially consumed foods
  • Home-canned goods
  • Food stored at unsafe temperatures

Food Safety

  • Ensure proper temperature control
  • Use clean, food-grade containers
  • Label all items with preparation date
  • List all ingredients and potential allergens
  • Follow proper hygiene during food handling
